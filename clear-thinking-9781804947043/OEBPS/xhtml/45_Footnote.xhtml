* I made it anonymous because, it turns out, three-letter agencies tend to frown on public profiles. Things have changed since those days. With all the trouble they have recruiting, you can have a public profile now. In fact, while job descriptions are vague, people often put the name of the agency they work for in their LinkedIn profile now. It’s important to realize when I started, we didn’t exist—there was no sign on the building. The idea of having any public profile was over a decade away.
