CHAPTER 2

THINK LIKE AN INVESTOR

Rule No. 1: Never lose money. Rule No. 2:
Never forget Rule No. 1.

– Warren Buffett

A great error for entrepreneurs is to not think like an investor. Whether you are just starting a business or have owned one for many years, you must think like an investor to maximize the value of your business. For most industries, you should take more of a long-term investor mindset like Warren Buffett would— patient and demanding but seeking the balance of profitability and growth. If you are in the tech industry, you must have a faster internal clock since your business can become irrelevant much faster.
