ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Greg Crabtree is a speaker, author, entrepreneur, and financial expert. Greg founded his own firm Crabtree, Rowe and Berger for the purpose of helping entrepreneurs build their own economic engine. After being named to the INC 5000 list for 2019, Greg’s firm merged with Carr, Riggs & Ingram CPAs and Advisors, a top 25 U.S. Accounting firm, to help broaden their impact on the entrepreneur community.

In 2011, Greg’s first book Simple Numbers, Straight Talk, Big Profits! shared his core principles of transforming a business into a wealth building engine. In 2014, Greg contributed a chapter to Verne Harnish’s book Scaling Up on how to improve profits through labor efficiency. Greg’s release of Simple Numbers 2.0: Rules for Smart Scaling reveals his team’s consulting insights gleaned during the decade following the first book’s publication. Greg shares his “Simple Numbers Process” to use smart scaling for maximizing return on investment for your business.

Greg is a frequent speaker to groups like the Entrepreneur’s Organization (EO), Scaling Up (Gazelles), Vistage, and ACETECH, and has presented in over 15 countries. Greg also chaired the EO@Wharton Executive Education program for EO.
