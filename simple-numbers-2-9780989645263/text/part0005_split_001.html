The Three Business Value Plays

Many entrepreneurs hear about companies that were sold and made them rich, and they get motivated to make the same thing happen for them. This can be a good thing if the entrepreneurâ€™s characteristics match those in the success story they heard, but unfortunately, most of the time they do not. Because I am often faced with this situation among my clients, I put some thought and research into how I can frame the situation to give them direction, and I came up with the following three business value plays:

  • Run to harvest
  • Harvest to premium sale
  • Build to sell

We have clients in all three of these scenarios. It is important to understand the differences so you can implement an effective strategy.
