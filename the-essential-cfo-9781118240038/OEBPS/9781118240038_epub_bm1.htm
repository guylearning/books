About the Author

BRUCE NOLOP RETIRED in 2011 after a 35-year career as an investment banker and corporate executive, including more than a decade as chief financial officer of Pitney Bowes and E*Trade Financial.

He has been a board member of Marsh & McLennan since January 2008 and currently serves on the audit, compliance and risk, finance, and corporate responsibility committees.

His education includes a B.A. in political science from the University of South Dakota, a J.D. from Stanford Law School, and an M.B.A. from the Stanford Graduate School of Business.

He lives in New York City with his wife, Susan, and has two sons, age 22 and 21.
