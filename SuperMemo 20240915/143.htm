Topic #151: From the assets side: Net worth (NW) = (Total Assets) TA - Current Liabilities (CL) - Long-Term Liabilities (LTL) A more pragmatic approach: All the values on the assets side .

From the assets side:

Net worth (NW) = (Total Assets) TA - Current Liabilities (CL) - Long-Term Liabilities (LTL)

A more pragmatic approach: All the values on the assets side of the balance sheet and deduct outstanding loans – anything left is shareholders’ money.
