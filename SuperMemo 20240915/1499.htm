Item #1,502: JavaScript: What characters are generally allowed in variable names?

JavaScript: What characters are generally allowed in variable names?
Latin characters (0-9, a-z, A-Z) and the underscore character.

JavaScript: Which variable name is valid: [firstOrder / 1stOrder]?
