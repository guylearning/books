    HTML: Which elements support `colspan` and `rowspan` attributes?
    <td> (table cell)  and <th> (table header).

    Item #1,934: [True / False]: HTML: The `colspan` and `rowspan` attributes are only used on the elements. (top)

    [True / False]: HTML: The `colspan` and `rowspan` attributes are only used on the <tr> elements.
    False.

    The colspan and rowspan attributes in HTML tables are used on <td> and <th> elements, not <tr> elements.
