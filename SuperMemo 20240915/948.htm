One thing I say to my clients all the time is that if you’re not paying taxes, there are only two possibilities: You didn’t make any income or you’re cheating. Paying taxes is a good thing. The higher your tax bill, the better your business is doing. This is your number one key performance indicator. You can’t create legitimate wealth from an operating business unless you pay taxes. Your business’s number one key performance indicator is this: How big a check did you write to the IRS this year?
