What is a balance sheet? It is simply an instant ‘snapshot’ of the assets used by the company and of the funds that are related to those assets. It is a static document relating to one point in time.

Topic #111: Further ‘snapshots’ will be taken at fixed intervals – months, quarters, years – to see how the assets and funds change with the passage of time. An analysis of these changes . (top)

Further ‘snapshots’ will be taken at fixed intervals – months, quarters, years – to see how the assets and funds change with the passage of time. An analysis of these changes gives crucial information about the company's activities over the period in question.
