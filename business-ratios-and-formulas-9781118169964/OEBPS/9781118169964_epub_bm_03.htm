About the Author

STEVEN BRAGG, CPA, CMA, CIA, CPIM, has been the chief financial officer or controller of four companies, as well as a consulting manager at Ernst & Young and auditor at Deloitte & Touche. He received a bachelor's degree in economics from the University of Maine, a master's degree in finance from Bentley College, and an MBA from Babson College. He has been a two-time president of the Colorado Mountain Club, is an avid alpine skier and mountain biker, and is a certified master diver. Mr. Bragg resides in Centennial, Colorado, with his wife and two daughters. He has published the following books through John Wiley & Sons:
