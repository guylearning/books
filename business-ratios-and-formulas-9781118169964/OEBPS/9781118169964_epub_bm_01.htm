Appendix

Measurement Summary

THIS APPENDIX CONTAINS ALL the measurements described in Chapters 2 through 14. It is intended to be a quick reference for the reader who needs to find a formula as soon as possible. Refer back to the related discussion in the main body of the book, however, for comments on alternative formula derivations or cautions on their use that may be pertinent.
