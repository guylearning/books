‘This year, we are asking you to reduce inventory assets. For instance, let’s look at product flow. When a product in a certain process is suspended, most people would consider immediately moving that product to the next process. We should all realize that such action is actually the shortest move and that the accumulation of such actions could save billions of yen. ... The saved amount naturally contributes to savings. Thus, cash flow operation enables us to locate, collect, and effectively use “hidden” cash reserves within the workplace to stengthen the company and enrich our collective livelihoods.’

—Masumi Fukuta
(President, Fujitsu Quantum Devices Limited)
