Overview

Gross profit is sales less costs of sales. This chapter examines these costs arithmetically. It looks at product and production costs and inventory calculations – how you analyse, interpret and forecast them. It then pulls them together with the sales forecast you made back in Chapter 10 to arrive at gross profit. The next chapter brings in the other costs to arrive at the all-important figure for net profit.

Prerequisites

If you were up to speed with the previous chapter, this one should be easy. When looking ahead, you will also need a sales forecast, which we discussed in Chapter 10.
